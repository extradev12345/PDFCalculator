Welcome to PDFCalculator Documentation

Your one-stop guide for the PDFCalculator app, your trusty tool for counting pages and estimating printing costs.

Features

  • Count total pages in PDF files within a folder.
  • Calculate printing costs with customizable price per sheet.
  • Duplex (double-sided) printing support.
  • Multiple pages per sheet printing options.
  • Clean, responsive, and theme toggleable UI.

How to Use

  1. Click “Browse” to select a folder containing your PDF files.
  2. The app will scan and show total pages and individual file counts.
  3. Set the price per sheet and printing options.
  4. Calculate the estimated printing cost instantly.
  5. Use the toggle button (top right) to switch between light and dark themes.

Pricing Calculation Details

Price is calculated based on the total number of sheets after accounting for pages per sheet and duplex printing options.

Sheets = ceil(total pages / pages per sheet / (duplex ? 2 : 1))

Estimated cost = price per sheet × sheets
